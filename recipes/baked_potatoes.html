Baked Potatoes

Description

An easy and fast recipe for baked potatoes.

Ingridients

  • 150g butter
  • 1 onion, hacked in blocks
  • potatoes, cooked and sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Let the butter melt in a frying pan with medium-high heat.
  2. Once the butter has melted, add the onions with a little bit of salt. Fry them, stirring regularly, until they are a little brown at the edges.
  3. Add the potato slices. Stir them regularly and fry them for a few minutes.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste - if you like, you may also add other spices.
  5. Once the potatoes have the wanted color and crispness, they are ready to be served. Enjoy!
